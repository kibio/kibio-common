Kibio is a simple, ready-to-use tool for digital media. Kibio maps, masks and warps video without all the fuss of existing solutions.

Load files. Make a playlist. Map your settings. Ready to go. Easy.

Kibio is designed for everybody to experiment and have fun with media. We’re excited to see Kibio and other open-source, openhardware tools get stronger. Collaboration is part of the solution. Let us know how it’s going. Let’s build better things together.
